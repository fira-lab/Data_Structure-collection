    The three pillars of observability

    The conversation about observability is often produced in the idea that observability is based on three main pillars, or data sources, that provide insight into a system:

    Logs
    Infrastructure and application activity information capturing a single point in time
    Metrics
    Details from application and cloud service performance
    Distributed traces
    Tracing requests within a distributed system
